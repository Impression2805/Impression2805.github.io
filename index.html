About me

I am a PhD student in the National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition at Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences advised by Cheng-Lin Liu and Xu-Yao Zhang. I obtained my B.E. from the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Tsinghua University in 2018.

My research interests include topics in pattern analysis and machine learning. My goal is to make machine learning systems more reliable and adaptable.
